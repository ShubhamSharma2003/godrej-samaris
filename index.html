Why Godrej Samaris

An address that appreciates with you

Sector 53 sits on Gurugram's premium Golf Course Road corridor — among the city's most sought-after and supply-constrained residential micro-markets.

Golf Course Road has matured into Gurugram's luxury spine, lined with marquee residential addresses, Grade-A offices and premium retail. Limited land and strict low-density planning keep new launches rare here — a key driver of long-term capital appreciation for early buyers.

Godrej Samaris is built for the end-user who refuses to compromise and the investor who reads location first. Walk-to-metro access at Sector 54 Chowk, a 30-minute airport connect, and a catchment of top schools, hospitals and workplaces make it equally strong for self-use and rental yield.

Developed by Godrej Properties. Founded in 1990, Godrej Properties is among India's most trusted real-estate developers, recognised for thoughtful architecture, quality construction, lush landscaping and timely delivery across leading cities.

Low-density living. With G+38 towers spread across 7.5 acres and roughly 80% open greens, Samaris trades crowding for breathing room — wide setbacks, dual clubhouses and curated landscapes at every turn.
